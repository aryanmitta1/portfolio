Leadership
Development

Becoming the kind of engineer others want to build with — through team leadership, mentoring, and the GLASS cohort seminar series that runs across all four years of the program.

What GLASS means by this

Leadership Development runs through the entire GLASS experience. The cohort seminar series builds professional, project-management, and leadership skills across all four years, paired with individualized coaching and workshops that help scholars clarify their goals and step into leadership roles on teams and in their communities.

My approach

  • Lead technically — own hard subsystems and coordinate the design decisions around them.
  • Earn trust through reliability: do what I say, document it, and make the team faster.
  • Develop people, not just deliverables — mentoring is leadership in its most durable form.
  • Use the GLASS seminars and coaching to sharpen how I communicate and make decisions under pressure.

Evidence & roles

  • Payload Electronics Lead — NYU Rogue Aerospace (NASA USLI): coordinated technical design decisions for telemetry and sensor integration.
  • TA Leader — EG-UY 1004: Communications, Inventory & Purchasing Leader for General Engineering.
  • GLASS cohort seminars: ongoing leadership and professional-development training across the program.
