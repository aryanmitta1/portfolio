03 — Featured Mission

Telemetry to NASA,
at 144 MHz.

NASA USLI · NYU Rogue Aerospace

Rocket Payload Telemetry System

Payload Electronics Lead

🚀

Led payload electronics for a NASA University Student Launch Initiative rocket — designing the full telemetry chain that transmitted live flight data to NASA's ground receiver, plus a mission-control GUI to arm, disarm, launch, and visualize data in real time.

What I built

  • Telemetry chain: Arduino + BMP390 + RTC → APRS over VHF radio.
  • Serial packet formatting & APRS message encoding (AFSK 1200 baud).
  • Radio / TNC chain: Mobilinkd TNC4 → Yaesu FT-70DR → NASA receiver.
  • Mission-control GUI: arm / disarm / launch + live data viz.
  • Battery, shielding & manual-override planning; NASA review docs.
